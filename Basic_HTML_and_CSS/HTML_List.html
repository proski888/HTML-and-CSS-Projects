Here is how you create a peanut butter and jelly sandwich:


  1. Grab 1st piece of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on 1st piece of bread
  3. Grab 2nd piece of bread
  4. Spread jelly on 2nd piece of bread
  5. Press both pieces of bread together (with the peanut butter and jelly sides touching!)

You can switch up the ingredients to make a unique sandwich. Here are some possible ideas:


  • Nutella
  • Honey
  • Bannanna
